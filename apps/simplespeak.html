SimpleSpeak

SimpleSpeak empowers users who have lost the ability to speak by making communication easy and intuitive.

The customizable interface allows you to add and organize your own phrases, as well as pre-made options, and speak them with a tap. With SimpleSpeak, communication is always at your fingertips.

Privacy Policy

SimpleSpeak respects your privacy and is designed to protect any information you provide. No personal information is collected, and we do not gather or share any data about your usage, location, or identity.
